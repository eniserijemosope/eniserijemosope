### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a Computer Engineering professional working at the intersection of **Data Science** and **Mobile App Development**. My focus is on building intelligent systems that turn raw data into actionable insight, and translating those insights into clean, functional mobile experiences with Flutter.

- 📊 **Data Scientist** — experience applying machine learning, speech-to-text, and LLM-based pipelines to real-world problems (e.g., automated QA scoring for customer support calls)
- 📱 **Flutter Developer** — building cross-platform mobile applications, from UI fundamentals to state management (GoRouter, Provider/Riverpod) and real data integration
- 🎯 **Currently focused on:** sharpening production-ready ML workflows and growing toward job-ready Flutter proficiency
- 🌍 **Long-term goal:** contribute to impactful open-source AI projects and pursue an advanced degree in AI/ML
- 🧠 **Philosophy:** practical, debuggable solutions over unnecessary abstraction
